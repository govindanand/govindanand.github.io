Vin Anand

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at University of California, Berkeley pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science (EECS) and Materials Science & Engineering. My interests lie in exploring the applications of machine learning and artificial intelligence on consumer technologies to build the next generation of products and features. In the past, I have worked on digital media projects to assess subscriber behavior -- characterizing user segments and building recommender systems through collaborative filtering and other advanced learning methods. I have also worked with International Airlines Group to introduce and implement new autonomous systems to increase the efficiency and passenger throughput at Heathrow Terminal 5.
