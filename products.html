Tap 2 Verify

Led identity verification for Capital One's Online Account Opening flow, replacing the multi-day Government ID process with a real-time tap-to-verify experience. Applicants confirm their identity via SMS link on their mobile device, eliminating friction for the 85k+ applicants per year previously routed to manual review and driving incremental account bookings at scale.

7k fewer manual reviews / month

Platform ML Infrastructure Internal tool

Versioning

Eliminated a key blocker to feature reuse on Capital One's ML Exchange. Data scientists were hacking version suffixes onto feature names, flooding the platform with near-duplicates and making reuse too risky. Introduced the first standardized versioning policy for data products, with automated change classification, tiered review flows, and a full audit trail that became the template for platform-wide data product lifecycle governance.

6x increase in feature reuse
5% → 30% reuse rate
0 → 1 platform versioning standard

Lineage

Built Feature Lineage to document the full lifecycle of data from origination through to model use, tracking both dataset-level flow and element-level field transformations across pipelines. Gave data scientists instant visibility into where features came from and how they were computed, enabling faster knowledge transfer, safer feature reuse, and audit-ready documentation for compliance requests.
